When I originally created this page and layout I had planned on filling this space with what would amount to an explicit definition of who I am, but either I lack the literary skills to perform such a task, I am too immodest to concede that it would be possible for myself, or it is, in fact, too daunting a task for anyone in such a short space to define a person.

So what I plan on doing instead is to try to keep organized on this site whatever I can about myself, and through this I hope to implicitly create a complex enough picture of who I am to both be accurate and fulfill my egotistical belief that it requires a fairly large level of complexity to create such a literary self-portrait.

Bear with me as I work to put what I can of myself up here. To try and create a sense of myself through a creative and self-driven purpose more than can be gathered (and amazingly accurately inferred) from the data my TiVo has on me.
