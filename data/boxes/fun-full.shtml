4/1/02 - G.O.D. So April Fools came and went again this year, but most cerntainly not without notice. This year we redid the entire dmoz user-interface to correspond with a Microsoft takeover of the ODP. We even had a clippy to help you edit, and gossip about the editors. With the current legal actions involving AOL and Microsoft, the higher ups got really mad at what was going on, and flipped out. Rich very quickly gave them my name and I was seriously worried about being fired. Bryn and Autumn were annoyed that even though they put in as much work as I did on the site, they weren't getting the same heat from the AOL execs. Anyway it all worked out well, and we were written up in several papers.
