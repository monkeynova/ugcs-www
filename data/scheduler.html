The Scheduler

Here is a nifty cgi script that will create a schedule for you in a usable format.
To use it, just type in your classes, seperated by new-lines, following the example...
CS 174a
CS 117a
CS 140a
CS 141a
Art 103
#Lunch MTWThF12 Ricketts
#Dinner MTWThF6 Ricketts

Information comes from this schedule.
